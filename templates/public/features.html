Platform Features

EcoNova AI provides a comprehensive suite of tools for citizens, municipal staff, and city administrators.

YOLOv8 AI Detection

Advanced computer vision automatically categorizes waste (plastic, organic, metal) and assesses the severity/priority directly from citizen photos.

Real-time GPS Heatmaps

Administrators can view live heatmaps of waste accumulation, allowing for optimized staff deployment and efficient route planning.

Automated Dispatch

The system automatically assigns critical complaints to the nearest available cleaning staff based on geographic proximity and current workload.

Deep Analytics

Comprehensive dashboards for admins providing insights into waste trends, staff performance, and environmental impact metrics over time.
